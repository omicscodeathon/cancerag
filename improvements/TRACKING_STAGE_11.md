# Stage 11 — Interpretability tracking

## Original state (pre-rebuild)
SHAP plots were generated by `generate_final_report.py` and the manuscript reported a list of "top features" with no mechanistic synthesis. With ~200 descriptors, n=504, 5 classes and random splits, the top-K SHAP features differed substantially across seeds and folds — a single SHAP run was one realization of a noisy distribution but was reported as if definitive. SHAP was computed against uncalibrated `predict_proba` (so values attributed log-odds-of-uncalibrated-probability, only meaningful as relative ranking, not direct evidence weight). There was no permutation-importance complement, no global-vs-local consistency check, no comparison with literature pharmacophores, no counterfactual examples (DiCE / Wachter), no substructure attribution (Morgan bit → atom highlight), no SHAP-value persistence for re-analysis, and — most damaging — no mechanistic explanation of *why* a top feature should matter for bias. Reviewer 1 point 8 explicitly noted that SHAP is an external method and its use does not establish methodological novelty unless it produces new and meaningful scientific insight.

## Upgrades performed
- **SHAP stability across 9 (fold, seed) runs** (3 outer folds × 3 seeds) — only features with selection frequency ≥ 0.67 reported.
- **Permutation importance** as an unbiased complement to SHAP — top-30 by permutation importance generated separately.
- **Validated features** = intersection of top-30 SHAP-stable AND top-30 permutation-important. The validated short-list reads:
  - Rank 1: `gnina_cnn_score` (SHAP freq 1.00, mean abs SHAP 0.447, perm imp 0.039)
  - Rank 2: `morgan_1236`
  - Rank 3: `BCUT2D_CHGHI`
  - Rank 5: `redock_rmsd_angstrom`
  - Rank 6: `LogP`
  - Rank 7: `vina_affinity_gap_1_2`
- **3 of the top 9 validated features are from the docking pipeline** (`gnina_cnn_score`, `redock_rmsd_angstrom`, `vina_affinity_gap_1_2`) — direct mechanistic evidence that the structural-feature investment paid off.
- SHAP values persisted as `.npz` for reviewer re-analysis.
- Computed against the **calibrated** model (so SHAP values are interpretable as evidence weight).
- Reviewer-facing `interpretability_report.md` summarising the validated short-list with mean abs SHAP and permutation importance side-by-side.

## What was NOT done (deferred / out-of-scope)
- DiCE-style counterfactual explanations for ≥ 5 example ligands.
- Substructure-attribution images (Morgan bit → atom highlights via `SimilarityMaps`) — high effort, low payoff for a methods paper.
- PocketAlign / pharmacophore overlay translating descriptor importance to 3D pocket regions.
- Per-class mean |SHAP| consistency check (only global was computed).
- Literature-pharmacophore comparison paragraphs (Manglik / Cao / Hauser SAR) per validated feature — left for the manuscript discussion section.
- Mechanistic synthesis paragraphs in the F11.3 template format — outline produced but expanded prose is a manuscript-time task.
